Up+ — Privacy Policy

Last updated / Дата обновления: 2026-06-22

Short version: Up+ does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Data we collect

None. Up+ has no accounts, no registration and no servers. Your training history, streak and settings are stored only locally on your device and never leave it.

2. Analytics, ads, tracking

Up+ contains no advertising, no analytics and no third-party tracking SDKs. We do not profile you and do not sell or share any data.

3. Notifications

Reminders are scheduled and shown locally on your device. No information is sent to us or anyone else to deliver them.

4. Donations

The optional “Support” button opens PayPal in your browser. If you choose to donate, the transaction is handled entirely by PayPal under PayPal’s Privacy Policy. We never receive your payment details.

5. Permissions

  • Notifications — to show your daily reminders.
  • Run at startup / wake — so reminders survive a device restart.

6. Children

Up+ is intended for adults and is not directed at children.

7. Deleting your data

Use “Reset all progress” in Settings, or simply uninstall the app — this removes all locally stored data.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy: mykyta.meshchaninov@gmail.com
